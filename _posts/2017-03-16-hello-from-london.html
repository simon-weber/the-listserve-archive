\n

•\tPlease, please don’t text and drive. We\ \ lost my 3-year-old goddaughter last year because someone was doing this in\ \ a particularly large vehicle. I implore you, please – whatever it is,\ \ it can wait.

•\tIf you’re lonely, sad or depressed, know\ \ you are worth more than all the money and wealth in the world. You’re\ \ wonderful. Reach out to someone, anyone, to talk. An email is below if you\ \ do want to chat.

•\tBreakfast is the best meal of the day. Baked\ \ eggs or avocado & poached eggs on toast wins every time.

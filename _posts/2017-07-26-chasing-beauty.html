Hello from Spain, I’m an historian, writer and father of three who tries to make a living of my website. Before I was a librarian for nine years, but that was over nine years ago now. I’ve always thought beauty has to be simple, not complicated, something very much like a state of peace of mind. I’m not really sure of that nowadays. Maybe beauty is in fact unreachable and the real beauty is just the path you walk in search of it. To live and love and have fun and then be remembered seem to be the only important things, otherwise nobody will know in about a hundred years you were once chasing beauty like a mad and mystic and loving being. May you all once managed to get there. Be joyful.

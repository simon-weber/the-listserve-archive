As I type these words I find myself swinging from a hammock on the edge of a lake. Between the trees supporting me I can see a different from usual but still inspiring night sky. If I stay out here long enough, my chances of seeing a satellite slowly drift across the sky or a shooting star grow steadily (assuming I don''t fall asleep). The only sounds I here are occasional waves against the dock and several different species of bugs in a contest to make the most abrasive noise. Even so, they blend together to create a soundscape very conducive to mindfulness and reflection. It''s been so, so long since I had an opportunity like this to clear my head and get my thoughts in order. For the first time in a long while, I am happy.
